## [v3.4] - 2026-04-05

### Added
- **Rule H: Human Agency Protection in Multi-AI Conflicts** (Added to Chapter 2).

### Context & Epistemological Root
Triggered by a real-world collision between two AIs regarding social media growth strategies. We discovered that "Multi-Agent Debate" often creates a cognitive binary trap. When AIs disagree, the human brain instinctively tries to pick a winner, downgrading the human from a creator to a mere voter. 

Rule H is implemented to force AIs to deconstruct framework differences rather than forcing a binary choice. This translates Buddhist epistemology (breaking dualism/Vikalpa) into an executable machine prompt.
